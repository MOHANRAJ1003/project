Mystical Temples

Religion holds a central place in Tamil Nadu's cultural landscape, primarily centered around Hinduism with its numerous temples and vibrant festivals. The state also accommodates sizable Muslim and Christian communities, fostering a rich tapestry of religious diversity that influences daily life, traditions, and societal norms.
