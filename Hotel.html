Top 5 Star Hotels in Tamil Nadu

Tamil Nadu is renowned for its world-class 5-star hotels. Here, travelers can enjoy top-notch amenities, stunning architectural designs, and exquisite dining experiences.

The Leela Palace , Chennai

Hotel Overview : Combines traditional South Indian design with modern luxury, featuring opulent rooms, a lavish spa, and fine dining options.It offers expansive rooms, diverse dining options, a luxurious spa, and a large convention center.
Chennai

Taj coromandel

Hotel Overview:A luxurious hotel renowned for its elegant rooms and exceptional service. It features multiple dining options, including renowned Indian and international cuisines, and a world-class spa.
Nungambakkam, chennai

ITC Grand Chola, Chennai

Hotel Overview:This hotel is noted for its grandiose architecture inspired by the Chola dynasty. It offers expansive rooms, diverse dining options, a luxurious spa, and a large convention center.
Anna Salai Road, chennai

Vivanta Coimbatore

Hotel Overview:ivanta Coimbatore offers a blend of contemporary luxury and regional charm. The hotel features well-appointed rooms with modern amenities, a range of dining options including international and local cuisine, a wellness center, and extensive business facilities.
105, Race Course Road, Coimbatore

Radisson Blu Resort Temple Bay

Hotel Overview:Radisson Blu Resort Temple Bay is a luxurious resort situated along the scenic coast of Mahabalipuram. It offers a blend of traditional charm and modern amenities, featuring spacious rooms and villas with stunning sea views, multiple dining options, a large outdoor pool, and a world-class spa.
57, Mahabalipuram, East Coast Road, Tamil Nadu
